# Function: hebrew2abs()

> **hebrew2abs**(`year`, `month`, `day`): `number`

Converts Hebrew date to R.D. (Rata Die) fixed days.
R.D. 1 is the imaginary date Monday, January 1, 1 on the Gregorian
Calendar.

## Parameters

• **year**: `number`

Hebrew year

• **month**: `number`

Hebrew month

• **day**: `number`

Hebrew date (1-30)

## Returns

`number`
